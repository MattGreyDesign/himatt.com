--- layout: page title: About permalink: /about/ priority: 0.9 intro: | Hi, I'm Matt Grey, a multi-disciplined designer particularly focussed around digital product design, user experience (UX), and usability. I aim to bridge the gap between design and technical implementation by working alongside developers to research, prototype and deliver products with effective and engaging user experiences. I currently work (and live) in Central London as a Product Designer (UX/UI) at [Clearscore](//www.clearscore.com/) Previously I was at [Acuris](//www.acuris.com/) where I worked on researching and designing the search journey for the flagship M&A deals database, and also built out a set of complex scalable design libraries to cover the company's six divisions. Before that I was a UI/UX designer across online shopping at [Tesco](//www.tesco.com/). I worked on the native mobile apps and website for groceries, a mobile phones ecommerce site, and helped develop the company-wide design libraries across the various platforms. Prior to that I was working in Southampton as a Designer and Consultant at [Alliants](//alliants.com) where I worked with clients such as [Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts](//fourseasons.com), [Travelodge](//travelodge.co.uk), [Ski Solutions](//skisolutions.com), [Babylon Health](//babylonhealth.com), [Sun Life Financial (Canada)](//www.sunlife.com), [CooperVision/Specsavers](//www.specsavers.co.uk/contact-lenses/coopervision), [Channel 5](//channel5.com), [butterfly.life](//itunes.apple.com/gb/app/butterfly-life/id1258575969), [VisitBritain](//www.visitbritain.com/gb/en), and [OpenJaw Technologies](//www.openjawtech.com). I design and prototype multi-platform websites and apps, sometimes developing branding and living styleguides if necessary. In the past I have also worked in print and illustration, producing album artwork, brochures and books.
